About Me

My First Post

QuestBridge National College Match Recipient

Aldrich Jad Sy Magpali

I am a current college student attending the #1 National Liberal Arts College in the United States on a full ride through the QuestBridge National College Match program. I offer peer-based guidance on college essays and high school planning.

📝 Important to know: I am not a professional college consultant. All feedback comes from my personal experience as a student who recently navigated the admissions process.

I believe that authenticity and vulnerability are key to crafting a compelling application. Many top colleges receive applicants with stellar stats: 4.0 GPA, perfect SAT or ACT. What differentiates you from others is your unique story.

I can best assist students from low-income/first-generation backgrounds who wish to pursue a STEM major.
